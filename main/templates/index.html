Welcome

Kshethra Vadya Kalamandalam is an organisation for preserving the disappearing Temple Arts. The aim is to teach the disappearing Temple Art forms to the younger generation and help and support out Gurunadhans who are in poor condition.

With the blessings of Dr. K. J Yesudas, other eminent Patron Personalities of Kshethra Vadya Kalamandalam and the co-operation of new generation who dedicated their lives for this Vadya Kalamandalam was officially announced.

We request you kind support and co-operation for Kshethra Vadya Kalamandalam
